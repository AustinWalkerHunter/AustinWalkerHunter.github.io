Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to enable location access?

No. Location access is optional. If enabled, the app can sort trail stops by walking distance and highlight nearby venues.

How do I log a whiskey tasting?

Open a trail stop and tap the option to log your pour. You can rate it, write notes, and optionally add a photo.

Why isn’t the nearest stop updating?

If location access is enabled, make sure your iPhone’s Location Services are turned on and the app has permission. You may also need to close and reopen the app.

Can I use the app over multiple days?

Yes. Your progress and tasting passport are saved automatically so you can complete the trail at your own pace.

How do I share my passport?

When you reach milestones, your passport can be shared as an image directly from the app.
