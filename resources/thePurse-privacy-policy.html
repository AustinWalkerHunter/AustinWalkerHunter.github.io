Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 1, 2026

At The Purse, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Introduction

The Purse ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes our practices regarding the collection and use of information through our mobile application (the "App").

By downloading, installing, or using The Purse, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our privacy practices, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Authentication Data: When you sign in using Apple Sign In or Google Sign In, we receive your email address and name
  • Profile Information: Display name, profile photo, handicap (if provided), and Venmo username
  • Tournament Data: Your tournament picks, entries, and group memberships
  • Communication: Messages, feedback, or support requests you send us

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique identifiers, mobile network information
  • Usage Data: Features used, actions taken, time spent in the App, crash reports
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how you interact with the App
  • Camera: If you choose to upload a profile photo, we access your camera or photo library

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Payment Processors: Venmo provides transaction confirmations and payment status
  • Public Data: PGA Tour leaderboard data and tournament information from ESPN

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App and its features
  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • To track tournament entries, picks, and standings
  • To calculate scores and earnings
  • To send you notifications about tournament updates, payment reminders, and results
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve the App
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To detect and prevent fraud or security issues

4.2 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand App usage, user behavior, and performance. See Google Analytics Privacy Policy.

Important: The Purse does not directly process or store payment information. All payments are processed through Venmo. When you pay or receive money through the App, you are directed to Venmo. Your payment information is subject to their privacy policy:

  • Venmo Privacy Policy

4.4 Apple Sign In & Google Sign In

We use Apple and Google authentication services. These services provide your basic profile information and are subject to their privacy policies. See Apple Privacy Policy and Google Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we are legally required to retain it. Historical tournament data and standings may be retained for competitive record purposes.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication via Apple Sign In and Google Sign In
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to personal data by authorized personnel only

However, no security system is impenetrable. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to Rectification: You can request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can limit how we use your data
